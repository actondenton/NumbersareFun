      Ascension — Number 1

      Ascension resets your Number 1 run: all extra hands are folded away, speed and cheapen upgrades are cleared, earned combo bonuses are cleared, turbo meter and unlock state reset, and short- and long-term objective checkmarks start fresh. You keep Ascension Essence forever. The Essence you gain scales with your total count at the moment you ascend — push higher before ascending to earn more. After your first ascension, open Global Overview to spend Essence on permanent skill branches (Tempo, Overdrive, …). You can respec for free any time.
